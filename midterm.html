Come to the best bargain in town and support your local library!
All net sale proceeds benefit the Palo Alto Library.

On the second weekend of every month, you are invited to browse over 50,000 gently used books, records, videos, magazines, and other treasures. These include donations from individuals, estates, and companies as well as previous library items.

Location & Hours

Spanning four meeting rooms at the Palo Alto Civic Center. Upon arriving, please look for the signs to direct you to each room.

Sale Locations Saturday and Sunday Hours
Main Room
Features the best finds in all genres of books, music and DVDs		 10am-4pm
Children’s Room
Includes books, non-English language materials, CDs, DVDs, and toys for preschool through early teens as well as books for parents and teachers		 9am-4pm
Bargain Rooms
Contains inexpensive items; On Sunday, everything in both rooms are 50% off or fill a grocery bag for $5		 9am-4pm

Prices

  • Hardbound books: $1 each
  • Paperback books: 50¢ each
  • Children’s and picture books: 50¢ or less
  • CDs and DVDs: $1 each
  • Gift certificates: available in $5 denominations

Additional Information

12 Item Limit

Because of our severe space limitations and concerns for everyone's health and safety, we will only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at a time. Therefore, as long as there is a line to enter the Main Room, there will be a 12-item purchase limit. There will also be a 12-item limit for the Children’s Room for the first hour Saturday morning.

Saturday early entry tickets

If you arrive early for the Main Room, line placement entry tickets will be available on a first come, first serve basis that will reserve your place in line. This will allow you to shop in the other rooms or enjoy the cafe before the Main Room opens. There is a max of 2 tickets per person and you must return to the line before the Main Room opens or you will lose your place and have to wait at the end.

Donations

Book sale donations will be accepted in the main meeting room until the Wednesday before the sale begins.
