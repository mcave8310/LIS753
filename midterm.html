Come to the best bargain in town:

The Friends of Palo Alto Library’s Monthly Used Book Sale

All net sale proceeds benefit the Palo Alto Library. Support your local library!

You are invited to to browse the Friends of Palo Alto Library’s Book Sale. On the second weekend of every month, 50,000 gently used books, records, videos, magazines, and other treasures are available for you to rediscover them. These include donations from individuals, estates, and companies as well as previous library items.

Location

Spanning four meeting rooms at the Palo Alto Civic Center. Upon arriving, please look for the signs to direct you to each room.

Most Prices

  • Hardbound books: $1 each
  • Paperback books: 50¢ each
  • Children’s and picture books: 50¢ or less
  • CDs and DVDs: $1 each
  • Gift certificates: in $5 denominations

More Information

12 Item Limit

Because of our severe space limitations and compelling issues of health and safety, we will only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at a time. Therefore, as long as there is a line to enter the Main Room, there will be a twelve (12) item purchase limit. There will also be a twelve (12) item limit for the Children’s Room on for the first hour Saturday morning.

Saturday early entry tickets

If you arrive early for the Main Room, entry tickets will be passed on a first come, first serve basis that will reserve your place in line. This will allow you to shop in the other rooms or enjoy the cafe. There is a max of two (2) tickets per person and you must return to the line before the Main Room opens or you will lose your place and have to wait at the end.

Donations

Book sale donations will be accepted in the main meeting room until the Wednesday before the sale begins.
