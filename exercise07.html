The hidden world of gardens

Welcome to my imaginary garden- as in a garden that I imagine is my own. This garden is located in Hartley Wintney, England.

Below are some groups that live in the garden:

Butterflies

This is an Eastern Tiger Swallowtail. It is generally found east of the Mississippi River in the United States and in some Mexican states.

The Eastern Monarch Butterfly can fly up to 2,500 miles from its breeding grounds in the United States and Canada to its hibernation grounds in central Mexico.

The Monarch primarily feeds on the milkweed plant. However, an increased use of herbicides has decreased the availabilty of this plant.

Grasshoppers

Grasshoppers are a common garden pest and are known to devour your prized basil plants.
